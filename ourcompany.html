OUR COMPANY

Verval Nusantara Hijau is a holistic advisory and assurance partner in the greenhouse gas ecosystem, with a focus on positive contributions to the environment and sustainable development.

Our Vision

To be come a landing company in advisory and assurance service within the greenhouse gas ecosystem, making a positive contribution to environmental conservation and sustainable development in indonesia by 2023.

Our Mission

Providing accurate, reliable, and internationally standardized greenhouse gas validation and verification service and economic valuation.

Developing professional competence and capacity in the environmental sector and carbon economic valuation ecosystem.

Contributing to sustainable economic development through the management of carbon economic value.
